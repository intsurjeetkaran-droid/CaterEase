WHAT IS CATEREASE?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

CaterEase is an online catering marketplace where:

  • Customers  → Find caterers, pick dishes, and book for their events
  • Providers  → List their food menu and manage incoming orders
  • Admin      → Oversees the whole platform

Think of it like a food delivery app, but for events like weddings,
birthday parties, corporate lunches, and more.

HOW TO OPEN THE APP
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Make sure the app is running (ask your developer to start it)
2. Open your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge — any works)
3. Go to:  http://localhost:3000

That's it! You'll see the CaterEase homepage.

HOW TO LOG IN
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Click "Login" in the top-right corner
2. Enter your email and password
3. Click "Sign In"

You will be taken to your personal dashboard automatically.

Don't have an account? Click "Sign Up" and fill in your details.
Choose your role: Customer or Catering Provider.

IF YOU ARE A CUSTOMER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1 — Browse Providers
  • After logging in, click "Browse Providers" or go to Providers in the menu
  • You'll see a list of all approved catering businesses
  • Each card shows the business name, location, and phone number

Step 2 — Pick Your Menu
  • Click "View Menu" on any provider card
  • You'll see all their available dishes with prices and descriptions
  • Click "Add" next to any dish to add it to your cart
  • Use the + and − buttons to change quantities
  • The total price updates automatically on the right side

Step 3 — Enter Event Details
  • On the right side order form, fill in:
    - Event Date (must be in the future)
    - Event Location (full address or venue name)
    - Your Phone Number (so caterer can contact you)
    - Guest Count (optional, helps caterer prepare)
    - Special Instructions (optional, any dietary requirements)

Step 4 — Place Your Order
  • Review your cart and event details
  • Click "Place Order" when you're ready
  • You'll be taken to your orders page

Step 5 — Make a Payment
  • Go to "My Orders" in the menu
  • Find your order and click "Make Payment"
  • Choose your payment method:
    
    💳 Online Payment:
    - You'll see the caterer's UPI ID and QR code
    - Click the copy button to copy the UPI ID
    - Pay using any UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
    - Optionally enter the transaction ID for reference
    - Click "Submit Payment"
    
    💵 Cash in Hand:
    - Select this if you'll pay the caterer directly in cash
    - The caterer will confirm when they receive the payment
    - Click "Submit Payment"
  
  • Enter the amount you want to pay
  • You can pay in parts (advance + remaining later)
  • Payment status will show:
    ✓ Paid — Payment confirmed
    ⏳ Pending — Waiting for confirmation
    Remaining — Amount still to be paid
  • Once fully paid, the order shows "✓ Fully paid"

Step 6 — Track Your Order
  • Your order will show one of these statuses:
    ⏳ Pending    — Waiting for the provider to accept
    ✅ Accepted   — Provider has confirmed your booking
    🔄 In Progress — Preparation is underway
    ✅ Completed  — Event done!
    ❌ Cancelled  — Order was cancelled

IF YOU ARE A CATERING PROVIDER
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Step 1 — Create Your Business Profile
  • After signing up as a Provider, go to your Dashboard
  • You'll be prompted to set up your business profile
  • Fill in: Business Name, Phone Number, Address
  • Submit and wait for Admin approval

  ⚠️  Important: You cannot add menu items or receive orders
      until the Admin approves your profile.

Step 2 — Configure Payment Details
  • Go to "Payment Settings" in the menu
  • Set up how customers will pay you:
    - UPI ID (e.g., yourname@paytm)
    - Account Holder Name
    - Bank Name
    - QR Code URL (upload your QR code to Imgur/Cloudinary and paste link)
  • Click "Save Payment Details"
  • Customers will see these details when they choose online payment

Step 3 — Add Your Menu Items
  • Once approved, go to "Manage Menus" in the top menu
  • Click "Add Item"
  • Fill in: 
    - Item Name (e.g., Paneer Tikka)
    - Category (e.g., Starters, Main Course, Desserts)
    - Price per serving
    - Description (optional, helps customers understand the dish)
  • Click "Save Item"
  • Repeat for all your dishes

Step 4 — Manage Incoming Orders
  • Go to "Orders" in the top menu
  • You'll see all orders placed by customers
  • Each order shows:
    - Customer name and email
    - Customer phone number (📞 highlighted in orange)
    - Event date and location
    - Guest count (if provided)
    - Special instructions/notes
    - Dishes ordered with quantities
    - Total amount
    - Payment status (Received, Pending, Remaining)
  
  • Use the buttons to update the order status:
    → Click "Mark as Accepted"   when you confirm the booking
    → Click "Mark as In Progress" when you start preparation
    → Click "Mark as Completed"  after the event is done
    → Click "Cancel"             if you cannot fulfill the order

Step 5 — Confirm Cash Payments
  • When a customer chooses "Cash in Hand" payment
  • You'll see a yellow box showing "Cash Payment Confirmation Required"
  • After receiving the cash from the customer:
    → Click "Received" to confirm you got the payment
    → Click "Failed" if the payment didn't happen
  • The payment status will update automatically

Step 6 — Contact Customers
  • Customer phone number is displayed prominently on each order
  • Event location shows exactly where you need to deliver/serve
  • Guest count helps you prepare the right quantity
  • Special notes contain any dietary requirements or preferences

Step 7 — View Your Earnings
  • Your Dashboard shows:
    - Pending Orders count
    - In Progress count
    - Completed Orders count
    - Total Earnings from completed orders

Dashboard
  • See total users, total orders, and total revenue at a glance
  • View pending provider approvals

Manage Users (Users page)
  • See every registered user (customers, providers, admins)

COMMON QUESTIONS
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

Q: I can't log in. What do I do?
A: Make sure you're using the correct email and password.
   Passwords are case-sensitive. If you forgot it, contact your admin.

Q: I placed an order but the provider hasn't responded.
A: The order will show "Pending" until the provider accepts it.
   You can contact the provider directly using their phone number
   shown on the provider card.

Q: Can I cancel my order?
A: Currently only the provider can cancel an order.
   Contact the provider directly using the phone number on your order
   to request a cancellation.

Q: I paid but the order still shows a remaining balance.
A: You may have made a partial payment. Click "Make Payment" again
   to pay the remaining amount. You can also check if your payment
   is still pending confirmation.

Q: I chose online payment but don't see the caterer's payment details.
A: The caterer may not have configured their payment details yet.
   Contact them directly or choose "Cash in Hand" payment method.

Q: How do I upload a QR code for payments?
A: Upload your QR code image to a free service like Imgur.com or
   Cloudinary.com, then copy the image URL and paste it in the
   Payment Settings page.

Q: Can I change my payment method after submitting?
A: No, once a payment is submitted, you cannot change the method.
   Contact the caterer directly if you need to make changes.

Q: My provider account says "pending" — what does that mean?
A: Your business profile is waiting for admin approval.
   Once approved, you can add menu items and receive orders.

Q: The page shows an error. What should I do?
A: Click the "Try again" button. If the problem continues,
   refresh the page or contact your developer.

Q: Why do I need to provide my phone number when ordering?
A: The caterer needs your phone number to coordinate delivery,
   confirm event details, and handle any last-minute changes.

Q: Can I edit my order after placing it?
A: Currently, you cannot edit orders after placing them.
   Contact the caterer directly using their phone number to
   discuss any changes.
